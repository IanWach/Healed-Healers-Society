Our Mission & Vision

Vision Statement

Restoring and equipping wounded healers to become whole, empowered, and effective agents of change—transforming lives and communities with renewed strength and purpose.

Mission Statement

To provide a transformative space for wounded healers to experience restoration, personal growth, and empowerment.

To restore, equip, and commission wounded healers into a journey of wholeness, leadership, and impact—transforming their pain into purpose and their experience into tools for healing others.

Our Programs

Comprehensive support through various initiatives designed to meet you where you are

Weekly Online

Healing Moments

Join us every Thursday for candid conversations on topics that matter. A safe space for sharing, learning, and growing together as a community.

  • Expert-led discussions
  • Peer support and connection
  • Practical healing strategies
  • Open to all seeking healing
Community Outreach

Mission Trips

Taking healing to communities across Kenya. Our missions bring professional mental health support and resources to underserved areas.

  • Professional counseling services
  • Community workshops
  • Resource distribution
  • Follow-up support systems
Children Support

Children's Home Visits

Monthly visits to children's homes providing emotional support, mentorship, and creating positive memories for vulnerable children.

  • Emotional support sessions
  • Educational activities
  • Mentorship programs
  • Recreational activities
Professional Training

Counselor Certification

Comprehensive training program for members who want to become certified counselors, turning their healed wounds into tools for helping others.

  • Professional curriculum
  • Hands-on practice
  • Certification upon completion
  • Ongoing supervision

📍 Molo Mission

First Major Outreach

Our inaugural mission to Molo brought professional mental health support to a community in need. We conducted individual counseling sessions, group therapy workshops, and established ongoing support systems.

📍 Kilifi Mission

Coastal Community Impact

Extending our reach to Kilifi, we addressed trauma, grief, and emotional challenges faced by coastal communities. Our team provided culturally sensitive counseling and established local support networks.

Revival Global Network

Part of Something Bigger

Healed Healers Society operates under the umbrella of Revival Global Network (RGN), an organization dedicated to bringing together young people for meaningful impact. Through RGN's support and vision, we're able to extend our reach and amplify our impact across communities.

RGN's mission is to raise a generation of Kingdom-minded believers who will transform societies and establish God's kingdom across the seven mountains of influence.

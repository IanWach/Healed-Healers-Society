## 🌱 About

**Healed Healers Society (HHS)** is a transformative mental health initiative founded by Expert Psychologist **David Njuguna**. We restore and equip wounded healers to become whole, empowered, and effective agents of change — transforming lives and communities with renewed strength and purpose.

Operating under **Revival Global Network (RGN)**, we bring together young people across Kenya with a shared mission to heal, grow, and make lasting impact.

### 🎯 Our Mission

**Turning Pain into Purpose**

To provide a transformative space for wounded healers to experience restoration, personal growth, and empowerment — equipping them to transform their pain into purpose and their experience into tools for healing others.

## 💚 Issues We Address

- Childhood Trauma
- Betrayal & Rejection
- Emotional & Sexual Abuse
- Shame & Self-Doubt
- Domestic Violence
- Self-Harm Recovery
- Divorce & Family Issues
- Loss & Grief
- Mental Health Challenges
- Poverty & Crime Impact

## 📋 Our Programs

### 1. **Healing Moments**
Weekly online sessions every Thursday for candid conversations, peer support, and expert guidance on mental health topics.

### 2. **Community Missions**
Professional mental health outreach to underserved communities across Kenya:
- ✅ Molo Mission (Completed)
- ✅ Kilifi Mission (Completed)
- 🔜 Upcoming missions

### 3. **Children's Home Visits**
Monthly visits providing emotional support, mentorship, and positive experiences for vulnerable children.

### 4. **Counselor Certification Program**
Professional training for HHS members to become certified counselors, turning their healed wounds into tools for helping others.
